Samsung Galaxy S23

The Samsung yearly smartphone refresh is officially upon us, with the Galaxy S23 series taking the reins in early 2023. The exciting next chapter in Samsung's phone story addresses multiple aspects of the Galaxy experience, bringing us a slightly different design (mostly for the better), much, much better performance and battery life, and finally, an tweaked camera experience that stretches the hardware and offers improved image quality.

Specification

  1. Dynamic AMOLED display

  3. Quad-camera setup on the backside
  4. Battery compatible with 45W Fast Charging
  5. On-screen fingerprint sensor
